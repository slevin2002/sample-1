INDIAN EXPRESS

"Massive Fire"

At least eight people died after a massive fire engulfed an electric bike showroom and spread to a lodge above it in Secunderabad late Monday,said the police Tuesday. All the victims were staying in a lodge located above the showroom, Deputy Commissioner of Police (DCP North Zone) Deepti Chandana said.

"When police reached here, the showroom and the lodge were engulfed in thick smoke. First responders and fire fighters were able to rescue a few persons using ladders. Some rescuers went up the stairs and brought down at least six persons who were unconcious. They had collapsed near the staircase due to some inhalation. The causes of the fire are yet to be ascertained. All the electric bikes in the showrroom were gutted," she said.
